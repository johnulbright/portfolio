About

I've spent the past fifteen years as a mathematics educator. Math and teaching have always been two of my passions.

In the past few years, since leaving my own classroom to become a math specialist for my school district, I've become equally passionate about the creation and best use of interactive, visual math programs to increase engagement, access, and understanding for students.

Looking forward, I find myself interested in broadening the ways of reaching both teachers and students. So I'm now in the process of completing a web development bootcamp. This website shows some of the work I've been completing as part of that, as well as a few other interests of mine.

About GeoGebra

GeoGebra is a dynamic mathematics program with broad international usage in math classrooms. There are many ways of using it, one of which is to author interactive applets for teachers to use with their students. I have been creating and sharing these with teachers from around the world since 2013.

See my website of GeoGebra resources for my entire library of applets, along with grade-level/content alignment and teaching notes. The collection of applets available here is a quick sample of some of my more popular work. Each of these applets has been used by teachers and students over 10,000 times.
