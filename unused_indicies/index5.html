GeoGebra

About GeoGebra

GeoGebra is a dynamic mathematics program with broad international usage in math classrooms. There are many ways of using it, one of which is to author interactive applets for teachers to use with their students. I have been creating and sharing these with teachers from around the world since 2013.

See my website of GeoGebra resources for my entire library of applets, along with grade-level/content alignment and teaching notes. The collection of applets available here is a quick sample of some of my more popular work. Each of these applets has been used by teachers and students over 10,000 times.

Adding Fractions - Visuals

Enter any two fractions to see their sum. Any mixed numbers must be entered as improper fractions.

Broken Clock

What time is it? Take successive guesses and click "More information" to put the pieces back on the clock.

Cash Register

Click "Start" then drag the coins onto the cash register to match the price of the item.

Close to 1000

Arrange the digits to make the sum as close to 1000 as possible.

Fold a Box

Adjust the dimesions of the box and the size of the corner squares. What patterns do you notice in the volume of the box?

Fraction Guesser

What fraction of the rectangle is shaded red?

Make a Linear Function

Drag the digits to the table in a way that makes a linear function.

Multiplying Fractions

Enter any to proper fractions to see them multiplied using an array approach.

What's the Number

A random number will be shown on the number line. Take successive guesses and click the "More info" button to see some helpful details.
